Welcome. This app can add earnings report reminders to your Google Calendar. Simply enter the stock symbols of your favorite publicly traded companies (separated by space or commas) in the text box and specify time horizon.

Notes:

  • Time horizon: choose 3 months for 1 report, 6 months for 2, and 12 months for 4.
  • Please note that the API from which the data is gathered has a request limit of 5/min and 500/day.
